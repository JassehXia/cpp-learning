# Recommended C++ Resources

To supplement your project-based learning, here are the best resources available:

## Documentation & References
- **[cppreference.com](https://en.cppreference.com/)**: The ultimate technical reference for C++.
- **[LearnCpp.com](https://www.learncpp.com/)**: The best free tutorial site for C++.

## Books (The Industry Standard)
- **A Tour of C++** by Bjarne Stroustrup (The creator of C++).
- **Effective C++** by Scott Meyers (Critical for senior-level engineering).

## YouTube Channels
- **[The Cherno C++ Suite](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLlrATfBNZ98dudnM48yfGUldqGD0S4FFb)**: Excellent for visual thinkers and game development.
- **[CppCon](https://www.youtube.com/user/CppCon)**: Watch the "Back to Basics" playlist for deep dives.

## Build Systems & Tools
- **[CMake Tutorial](https://cmake.org/cmake/help/latest/guide/tutorial/index.html)**: Learn how to manage project builds.
